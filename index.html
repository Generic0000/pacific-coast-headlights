The Smart Decision

Why Restore Instead
of Replace?

New OEM headlights run $300–$1,200+ per side at the dealer. Cheap Amazon replacements yellow faster than your originals and often fail within a year. Here's the truth.

🏭
Keep Your OEM Headlights
Your factory headlights are precision-engineered for your exact vehicle — perfect beam pattern, factory seal, and exact fitment. OEM polycarbonate is higher grade than any aftermarket replacement. Restoring what you have keeps all of that.
⚠️
Cheap Amazon Replacements Yellow Even Faster
Budget aftermarket headlights use thinner polycarbonate with inferior UV coating. Most start hazing within 12–18 months in the California sun — faster than your originals did. You'd be right back where you started, just $150–$400 poorer.
💧
Aftermarket Seals Fail — Water Gets In
Low-cost housings have poor gasket quality. Moisture intrusion fogs the lens from the inside, destroys the reflector, and shorts your bulbs. Once water's in, the unit is trash. Your original factory-sealed housing has zero risk of this when properly restored.
💰
Save Hundreds — Same-Day Results
Dealer OEM replacement: $400–$1,200+ per side. Budget Amazon pair: $100–$300 that fades in a year. Our restoration: $149 flat, both headlights, we come to you. Most customers save $500 or more and get results that last 3–5 years.
